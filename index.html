How to guide

Here we have broken down the main steps, to help guide you with your purchase.
01

Set up HTR Wallet

  1. Download the official Hathor Wallet from the app store and follow these videos.
  2. Keep note of your HTR Wallet address as you will need it in the next steps. This is not your KuCoin address.
02

Buy HTR

  1. Buy HTR from KuCoin.
  2. Withdraw from Kucoin to your HTR wallet address, this should take a few seconds. Occasionally the network can be congested and may take longer than expected.
  3. Continue to the next step once you have received the HTR in your official wallet.
03

Mint NFT / Send HTR

  1. Using the form at the top of this page, select how many NFTs you wish to buy
  2. Enter your official HTR wallet address, and press continue.
  3. Send the full amount of HTR to the deposit address shown to you.
04

Register NFT

  1. Once the transaction has completed, add the string to your official HTR Wallet.
  2. To view your NFT you can use the Mummy Viewer page.
